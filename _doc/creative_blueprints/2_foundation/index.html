The Problem

Human tutors cost $30–$80/hr

Most learners can't afford consistent practice with a real tutor.

Existing apps ignore speaking

Most language apps focus on reading and writing — not the speaking that matters most for real-world use.

Fear of judgment blocks progress

Learners avoid speaking practice because they feel embarrassed making mistakes in front of others.

✓

The Solution

AI voice conversations, 24/7

Natural spoken practice with an always-available AI tutor — no scheduling, no cost per session.

Non-interruptive, personalized feedback

Pronunciation, grammar, and vocabulary coaching delivered after each turn — conversation flow preserved, confidence intact.

Adaptive learning, zero judgment

AI meets every learner at their current level (Beginner → Advanced) and grows with them in a fully safe, encouraging environment.
