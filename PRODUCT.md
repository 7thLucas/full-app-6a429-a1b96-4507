# SpeakFlow — Voice-First AI English Speaking Tutor

## What It Is
SpeakFlow is a voice-first AI English speaking tutor app. Users practice spoken English in natural, judgment-free AI conversations. Real-time AI responses, post-turn pronunciation feedback, adaptive learning paths, and gamified streaks turn daily speaking practice into a habit.

## Target Users
Non-native English speakers worldwide — from beginners afraid to open their mouths to intermediate speakers who want to sound more natural and confident.

## Core Problem
Fear of judgment stops people from practicing spoken English. SpeakFlow creates a safe, patient, encouraging space where mistakes are welcomed as learning moments.

## Brand
- **App name:** SpeakFlow
- **Tone:** Patient, encouraging, motivating — never condescending
- **Positioning:** Your AI speaking coach, always available, never judgmental

## Core Features

### 1. AI Voice Tutor
Natural back-and-forth spoken conversations powered by AI. Real-time responses keep the conversation flowing. Users speak; SpeakFlow listens, responds, and keeps going.

### 2. Pronunciation Feedback
Non-interruptive. Delivered after each conversation turn — not mid-sentence. Specific, actionable tips (e.g. "Your 'th' sounds like 'd' — try touching your tongue to your upper teeth").

### 3. Personalized Learning Path
Proficiency assessment on first use → adaptive plan. AI adjusts difficulty based on performance. Tracks weak areas and revisits them.

### 4. Practice Scenarios
Structured real-world scenarios:
- Food ordering (café, restaurant)
- Travel (airport, hotel, directions)
- Job interviews
- Business presentations
- Small talk / social conversations
- Phone calls

### 5. Grammar & Vocabulary Coaching
In-context corrections shown after each turn. Simple explanations, no jargon. New vocabulary introduced naturally in conversation.

### 6. Conversation History
Full session transcripts. Review what was said, what was corrected, what vocabulary was learned. Searchable history.

### 7. Daily Challenges
Streak-based exercises. One challenge per day — a short scenario, a pronunciation drill, or a vocab challenge. Completing it keeps the streak alive.

### 8. Progress Tracking
- Pronunciation score (0–100)
- Grammar score (0–100)
- Vocabulary growth (words learned over time)
- Sessions completed
- Total speaking time

### 9. Achievement Badges
Milestone rewards: first conversation, 7-day streak, 100 words learned, pronunciation breakthrough, etc.

### 10. AI Coach Notifications
- Daily reminders
- Session summaries
- Inactivity nudges ("You haven't practiced in 3 days — let's keep your streak alive!")
- Weekly progress reports

## Dashboard
- Daily speaking goal + progress bar
- Proficiency level (Beginner / Intermediate / Advanced)
- Speaking streak counter
- Weekly progress chart (sessions per day)
- Pronunciation score, grammar score, vocabulary score — as visual indicators
- Recently learned words panel
- AI insights + personalized recommendations
- Quick-start buttons for scenarios

## Strategic Principles
- **Voice-first:** Everything serves speaking practice. Text is secondary.
- **Non-interruptive feedback:** Corrections come after turns, never during.
- **Adaptive difficulty:** AI grows with the user — never too easy, never overwhelming.
- **Judgment-free:** Safe space to make mistakes. Positive framing always.
- **Streak/gamification:** Daily habit formation through streaks, badges, and goals.
